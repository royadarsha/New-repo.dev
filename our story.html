Our Story

An evolution defined by precision, craftsmanship, and a relentless pursuit of the extraordinary.

Our Genesis

Modenity was founded to redefine the B2B surface industry by shifting the focus from sales volume to value creation. We bridge the gap between global trends and local needs, delivering fashion-forward designs and superior quality that truly understand the Indian construction ecosystem.



Our journey began by studying the local market. We saw a clear gap: while India is full of large tiles with repetitive designs, Europe is innovating with detailed, small-format looks.

We stepped in to bridge this design divide. Operating on an asset-light model, we retain full control over R&D and design with strategic manufacturing partners. We moved away from simple "trading" to focus on curation, bringing unique small-format tiles to the Indian market.

But design isn't enough. We know the market needs consistency, transparency, and trust. For an Architect or Builder, a tile is not just a product—it is a commitment to their client.

Modenity exists to honor that commitment. We bring European stylesclean lines and modern textures—made for the practical realities of Indian construction. We know Indian properties are built for generations. So, our surfaces don't just offer global fashion today, but trusted performance that lasts for decades.
